\begin{abstract}
PATV-X is a modular research suite for explainable video anomaly detection, focusing on physics-aware, temporal, and semantic consistency metrics. It supports multi-level evaluation, ablation studies, and robust benchmarking for video forensics and deepfake detection. This paper presents the architecture, methodology, experimental evaluation, and key findings of PATV-X. All research and experiments were conducted in 2026.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
Video forensics and deepfake detection require explainable, robust, and physics-aware metrics. PATV-X provides a unified framework for evaluating temporal, semantic, and physical consistency in video data, supporting both research and practical deployment.

\section{Related Work}
% Add references to video forensics, deepfake detection, explainable AI, temporal consistency, physics-based metrics, etc.
Recent work in video forensics and explainable AI highlights the need for multi-level, interpretable metrics. PATV-X builds on these by integrating temporal, semantic, and physics-based analysis.

\section{System Architecture}
\subsection{Core Components}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Feature Extraction:} Temporal, semantic, and physics-based features
    \item \textbf{Ablation Framework:} Modular ablation studies for metric impact
    \item \textbf{Benchmarking Pipelines:} Automated scripts for reproducible evaluation
    \item \textbf{Multi-Level Metrics:} L1 (motion/physics), L2 (object/scene), L3 (semantic/face/context)
    \item \textbf{Visualization:} Explainable outputs and reporting
\end{itemize}

\section{Methodology}
\subsection{Evaluation Protocol}
Experiments follow a rigorous protocol: multi-dataset evaluation, ablation, statistical analysis, and reporting of all key metrics. All scripts are reproducible and open-source.

\subsection{Metrics}
Key metrics include semantic score, color drift, edge stability, texture consistency, physics score, and anomaly detection rates.

\section{Experimental Evaluation}
\subsection{Datasets}
Experiments cover synthetic and real video datasets, with pipelines for ablation and benchmarking.

\subsection{Results}
% Summarize key results, e.g., ablation impact, metric robustness, explainability, etc.
PATV-X demonstrates robust detection of temporal and semantic inconsistencies, interpretable metric outputs, and effective ablation analysis.

\section{Limitations and Future Work}
Current limitations include limited real-world deployment and focus on academic datasets. Future work includes operational deployment, adversarial robustness, and integration with new video forensics benchmarks.

\section{Conclusion}
PATV-X provides a reproducible, extensible platform for explainable video anomaly detection, supporting robust benchmarking and practical extensions.
